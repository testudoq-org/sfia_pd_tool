SFIA POSITION DESCRIPTION Generator

Instructions

Welcome to the PD generator tool, built upon the SFIA 8 Framework.

To initiate PD generation, choose the desired skill set levels.

Description of any skill is available by hovering over it, and for any skill level, hover over its respective checkbox.

Your selected choices will be formatted and provided at the page's end. Copy and paste it into a word processor for formatting preservation.

You can also export your selection as a comma-separated value (CSV) document or HTML, automatically downloaded for you. For optimal use, consider an up-to-date version of Chrome or Firefox, as some browsers may have limitations. In case of an incorrectly named file, rest assured the content remains unchanged.

To save your summary for future reference, bookmark the page. The URL bookmark retains necessary information and updates your selection upon your return.
